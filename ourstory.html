Our Legacy of Craftsmanship

A journey through time, passion, and exquisite jewelry

Scroll to Explore
1985

The Beginning

Founded in a small workshop in Florence, Italy, Luxuria began as a passion project of master jeweler Giovanni Moretti. With just two apprentices, he set out to create jewelry that would stand the test of time.

Our original workshop in Florence
1992

First Collection

Our debut collection, "Eternal Rose," featured handcrafted floral motifs that quickly gained recognition among jewelry connoisseurs. This collection established our signature style of intricate detailing.

The Eternal Rose collection
2001

New York Boutique

We opened our first international boutique on Fifth Avenue in New York City, bringing European craftsmanship to American clientele. This marked the beginning of our global presence.

Our New York flagship store
2010

Ethical Sourcing

Committed to responsible practices, we established direct partnerships with ethical mines and became a certified member of the Responsible Jewellery Council, ensuring conflict-free diamonds and fair labor practices.

Our ethical mining partners
2020

Modern Renaissance

Celebrating 35 years, we launched our "Modern Renaissance" collection, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design. This collection represents our commitment to innovation while honoring our heritage.

Modern Renaissance pieces

Our Philosophy

At Luxuria, we believe jewelry should tell a story - your story. Each piece is designed to become a cherished heirloom, passed down through generations while retaining its beauty and significance.

We combine time-honored craftsmanship with contemporary design, creating pieces that are both timeless and modern. Every detail matters, from the ethical sourcing of materials to the final polish.
