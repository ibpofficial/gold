Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached.

Non-returnable items: Custom or personalized items, earrings for hygiene reasons, and items marked as final sale.

How to Return

  1. 1 Initiate your return through our Returns Portal
  2. 2 Print your prepaid return label
  3. 3 Package your item securely
  4. 4 Drop off at any UPS location

Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. You will receive an email notification once your refund has been issued.

Original shipping costs are non-refundable. For exchanges, we'll cover the cost of shipping the new item to you.
