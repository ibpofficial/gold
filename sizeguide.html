Ring Size Chart

Measure your finger or compare to a ring that fits you well.

Measure Your Finger

  1. Wrap a strip of paper around your finger
  2. Mark where the paper overlaps
  3. Measure the length in millimeters
  4. Match to our size chart

Compare to Existing Ring

  1. Place a ring that fits well on the chart
  2. Match the inner diameter to our sizes
  3. Use the closest match
US Size UK Size Diameter (mm) Circumference (mm)
4 H½ 14.9 46.8
5 J½ 15.7 49.3
6 L½ 16.5 51.9
7 N½ 17.3 54.4
8 P½ 18.1 56.9

Ring Sizing Tips

  • Measure your finger at the end of the day when it's likely to be its largest
  • Consider the width of the band - wider bands may require a larger size
  • Your ring should fit snugly but slide over your knuckle with some resistance
  • Fingers change size with temperature and time - consider seasonal variations
Medium 18cm

Bracelet Size Chart

Measure your wrist to find your perfect bracelet size.

Measure Your Wrist

  1. Wrap a flexible tape measure around your wrist
  2. Note the measurement in centimeters
  3. Add 1-2cm for a comfortable fit
  4. Match to our size chart
Size Wrist Circumference Bracelet Length
Small 14-15cm 16cm
Medium 16-17cm 18cm
Large 18-19cm 20cm
X-Large 20-21cm 22cm

Bracelet Sizing Tips

  • Measure your wrist where you normally wear bracelets
  • For bangles, measure across the widest part of your hand
  • Consider the style - chain bracelets can often be adjusted
  • For a snug fit, add 1cm to your wrist measurement
  • For a loose fit, add 2cm to your wrist measurement
18" Collarbone

Necklace Size Chart

Determine where you'd like your necklace to sit.

Measure Your Neck

  1. Use a flexible tape measure around your neck
  2. Add 2-4 inches depending on desired fit
  3. Match to our standard lengths
Length Position Best For
14" Choker Fits snugly around the neck
16" Neckline Rests at base of the neck
18" Collarbone Most common length
20" Below Collarbone Pairs well with pendants
22-24" Chest Opera length

Necklace Sizing Tips

  • Consider your neckline - different outfits may require different lengths
  • For pendants, allow extra length for the pendant to hang properly
  • Layered necklaces should have at least 2" difference between lengths
  • Most people find 16-18" to be the most versatile length
